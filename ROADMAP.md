# 🗺 Roadmap

This document outlines the future plans for the Receipt Manager project.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-03 (Current)
- **AI-Powered Scanning**: Gemini AI extracts receipt data from images/PDFs.
- **Expense Splitting**: Assign items to different people to calculate individual shares.
- **Cloud Sync**: Google Drive and Google Sheets integration.
- **Multi-language Support**: English and Hebrew (RTL).
- **AI Chat Assistant**: Natural language commands for session management.
- **Real-time History**: Firebase Firestore integration for session persistence.
- **Comprehensive Documentation**: README, DEVELOPMENT, FIREBASE, TESTING, CONTRIBUTING, CHANGELOG, and LICENSE.

## [1.1.0] - Future (Planned)
- **Multi-User Collaboration**: Allow multiple users to join a session and assign items in real-time.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Visualize spending patterns with charts and graphs.
- **Export to PDF**: Generate a summary PDF for each session.
- **Mobile App**: Develop a mobile app for on-the-go scanning.
- **Integration with Accounting Software**: Export data to QuickBooks, Xero, etc.
- **Improved AI Parsing**: Support for more complex receipts and handwriting.

## [2.0.0] - Long-term (Vision)
- **Automatic Expense Categorization**: AI-driven categorization based on historical data.
- **Budgeting & Alerts**: Set budgets and receive alerts when spending exceeds limits.
- **Receipt Archiving**: Long-term storage and search for all scanned receipts.
- **AI Financial Advisor**: Personalized financial advice based on spending habits.
